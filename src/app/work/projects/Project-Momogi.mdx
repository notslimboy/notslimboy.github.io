## Overview

**Momogi Roblox** is an engaging tower defense game on the Roblox platform, created as part of an interactive promotional campaign.

Players can purchase special Momogi _"Jelajah Nusantara"_ products in the real world to discover unique, hidden redeemable codes. Entering these unique codes in-game unlocks exclusive characters, boosts, and resources to help defend your base! 

Play the game, collect all the unique codes to build and complete your ultimate collection deck, and stand a chance to win exciting real-world prizes!
